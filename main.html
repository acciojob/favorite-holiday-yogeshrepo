Waynad


The Waynad is famous for wildlife and its spice plantations. Wayanad is part of a forest reserve, located on the border of Tamil Nadu and Kerala. Because of it wild green coverage it offers great scenic beauty along with the experience of seeing wildlife.

  1. Kuruva Island
  2. Chembra Peak
  3. Edakkal Cave
  4. Muthanga Wildlife Sanctuary
  5. Soochippara Waterfalls
  • Fish Moilee.
  • Appam & Chicken stew.
  • Nadan Beef Fry.
  • Payasam
  • Karimeen Pollichathu.
